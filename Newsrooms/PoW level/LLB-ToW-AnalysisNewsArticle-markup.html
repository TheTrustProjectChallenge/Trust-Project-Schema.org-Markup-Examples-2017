Here is a demonstration of what can be done right now about citations and references

Citations and References are an indicator for Trust Project. It consists in displaying links to the sources that have been used either as a source of inspiration for a story or as a direct source of information - aka a reference - to write the story.

It consists in a list of links that are presented in the stories and which point at the needed sources.

Here is the way the newsroom will handle it in the current CMS, prior to the go live of the new CMS. The new CMS proposes a richer user experience that allows journalists to include as a content element in a story a list of links called Citations and References. That content element will be given a specific layout.

Methods:

This story has been initiated after La Libre Belgique decided to apply to become part of Trust Project. Weekly meetings took place with Trust Project executive team to gather and consolidate all the data required to be compliant: they involved Sally and the other representatives of the publishers from the Cohort IV of Trust Project. Those gatherings aimed at clearing out the objectives of each indicator, the manner to implement them and eventually orchestrate the marketing campaign at launch.
